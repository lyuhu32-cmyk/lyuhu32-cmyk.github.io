Flame Retardant Coating for Wood

Professional Fire Resistant Paint for Timber and Wooden Structures

Product Overview

Our flame retardant coating for wood is a water-based fire protection solution designed to improve fire resistance performance of timber, plywood, decorative panels, and other wooden construction materials.

The coating reduces flame spread and enhances fire safety compliance in residential, commercial, and public building projects.

Technical Specifications

  • Base type: Water-based
  • Application method: Spray / Brush / Roller
  • Suitable substrates: Timber, plywood, MDF panels
  • Low VOC formulation
  • Finish: Transparent or white (customizable)

Application Areas

  • Wooden interior decoration
  • Public buildings
  • Wooden ceiling systems
  • Timber houses
  • Commercial projects
