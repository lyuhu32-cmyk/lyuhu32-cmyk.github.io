Frequently Asked Questions

What is fire retardant coating?

Fire retardant coating is a protective material applied to surfaces to reduce flame spread and improve fire resistance performance.

How does intumescent coating work?

Intumescent coatings expand under heat and form a protective insulating layer that shields structural steel.

Is wood flame retardant coating durable?

Yes, properly applied coatings provide long-term fire resistance performance.

What fire ratings are available?

Typical fire resistance ratings range from 30 to 120 minutes depending on thickness and structure.
