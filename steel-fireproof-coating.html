Intumescent Fireproof Paint for Steel Structure

Professional Fire Resistant Coating for Structural Steel Protection

Product Overview

Our intumescent fireproof paint for steel structures is designed to provide advanced fire resistance protection for industrial buildings, steel warehouses, commercial facilities, and infrastructure projects.

Under high temperature exposure, the coating expands to form a thick insulating char layer that delays heat transfer and protects structural steel from collapse.

  • Application method: Spray / Brush / Roller
  • Surface: Structural steel beams and columns
  • Drying time: Fast surface drying under normal temperature
