Product Overview

Our steel fireproof coating is an advanced intumescent fire protection system formulated for structural steel applications. When exposed to high temperatures, the coating expands to form a stable insulating char layer, significantly delaying temperature rise and preventing structural collapse.

Suitable for commercial buildings, industrial facilities, stadiums, airports, and infrastructure projects, the system complies with international fire resistance standards.

Key Features

Up to 3 Hours Fire Resistance

Thin Film Intumescent Technology

Suitable for both interior and exterior protected steel structures.
