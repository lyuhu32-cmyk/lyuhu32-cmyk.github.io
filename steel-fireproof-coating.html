Product Overview

Our steel fireproof coating expands under high temperature conditions to create a thermal insulation barrier, preventing structural steel from reaching critical failure temperatures. The system ensures building safety compliance with international fire resistance standards.

It is widely used in commercial buildings, industrial plants, warehouses, public infrastructure, and high-rise structures requiring certified passive fire protection.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is intumescent steel fireproof coating?

Intumescent steel fireproof coating is a thin film protective material that expands when exposed to high temperatures, forming an insulating char layer that protects structural steel.

How many hours of fire resistance can it provide?

The coating system can provide up to 3 hours of certified fire resistance depending on thickness and design requirements.

Is it suitable for exterior use?

Yes. When combined with approved primers and protective topcoats, it can be applied to protected exterior steel structures.
