Steel Fireproof Coating Systems

Intumescent steel fireproof coatings are designed to protect load-bearing structural steel members during fire exposure. When subjected to high temperatures, the coating expands to form an insulating char layer that delays temperature rise in the steel substrate.

Fire resistance is verified through standardized testing procedures such as ASTM E119 fire testing standard.
