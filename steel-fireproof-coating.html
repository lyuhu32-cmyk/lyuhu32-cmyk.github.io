What Is Intumescent Steel Fireproof Coating?

Intumescent steel fireproof coating is a specialized fire protection material designed to protect structural steel elements from high-temperature exposure during fire incidents.

When exposed to extreme heat, the coating expands and forms a thick insulating char layer. This thermal barrier significantly delays heat transfer to steel substrates, maintaining structural integrity and load-bearing capacity.

Fire Resistance Performance

  • Fire resistance ratings from 30 minutes to 120 minutes
  • Suitable for commercial and industrial buildings
  • Tested under controlled fire exposure conditions
  • Optimized coating thickness based on steel section factor

Key Technical Advantages

  • Water-based or solvent-based systems available
  • Strong adhesion to primed structural steel
  • Low VOC options for environmental compliance
  • Smooth finish suitable for architectural projects
  • Compatible with topcoat systems

Applications

  • Steel frame buildings
  • Industrial plants
  • Commercial complexes
  • Warehouses
  • Public infrastructure projects
