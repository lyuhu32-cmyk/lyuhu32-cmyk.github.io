Product Overview

Our steel fireproof coating is an advanced intumescent fire protection system designed for structural steel beams, columns, and load-bearing frameworks. When exposed to high temperatures, the coating expands to form a thick insulating char layer that protects the steel substrate.

Fire Resistance Ratings

  • 1 Hour Fire Resistance
  • 2 Hour Fire Resistance
  • 3 Hour Fire Resistance

For detailed fire resistance standards and testing methods, please refer to our Steel Fire Resistance Ratings Guide.

How Intumescent Coatings Work

Intumescent coatings react under heat exposure. The chemical reaction forms a carbonaceous char layer, significantly reducing heat transfer to structural steel.
