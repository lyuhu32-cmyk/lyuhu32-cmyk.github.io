Project Case Study: Transparent Fire Protection Success

Premium fire protection solutions combining safety, environmental responsibility, and architectural aesthetics.

Beijing 2024 National Stadium Equestrian Event

During the 2024 major equestrian competition hosted at the iconic National Stadium in Beijing, our transparent intumescent fire protection coating was selected for key structural wood and steel elements.

Project Requirements

  • Maintain original architectural appearance
  • Provide certified fire resistance performance
  • Low odor & environmentally friendly
  • Fast curing for event schedule deadline

Solution Provided

Our water-based transparent intumescent coating delivered up to 60-minute fire resistance while preserving the natural material texture. The ultra-clear finish ensured that architectural aesthetics were fully retained.

Macao 2025 – 25th Anniversary Landmark Project

In celebration of the 25th Anniversary of Macao's return, our environmentally friendly transparent fire-retardant coating was selected again for a high-visibility landmark structure.

Why Selected Again?

  • Zero strong odor during application
  • Crystal-clear transparent finish
  • Certified fire rating performance
  • Compliance with green building standards

The project required both high fire safety standards and architectural transparency. Our advanced intumescent formulation expanded under heat exposure, forming an insulating char layer while remaining invisible under normal conditions.

Project Results

  • Improved fire safety performance
  • Preserved original design aesthetics
  • Environmentally responsible solution
  • Successful delivery under tight deadlines
