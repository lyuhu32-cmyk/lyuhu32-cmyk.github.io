ASTM E119 Fire Testing Standard Explained

ASTM E119 is a standard fire-resistance test method used to evaluate the performance of building assemblies and structural elements under controlled fire exposure conditions.

Standard Time-Temperature Curve

The test follows a prescribed furnace temperature curve, reaching approximately 1000°C within the first 2 hours.

Failure Criteria

  • Structural collapse
  • Excessive temperature rise
  • Integrity failure

Application in Structural Steel Fireproof Coatings

ASTM E119 test data provides the performance basis for determining required thickness of structural steel fireproof coating systems.
